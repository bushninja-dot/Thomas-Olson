Introduction

This project explores two dual spaces that govern linear dynamical systems: the function space of eigenfunctions and the spectrum of eigenvalues. We'll develop intuition, algebraic formulas, and visualizations for how matrices evolve in time, how determinants behave when eigenvalues lie on the unit circle, and how differentiating either eigenfunctions or characteristic polynomials produces fundamentally different effects.
